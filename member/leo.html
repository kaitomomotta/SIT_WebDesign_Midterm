Olá, sou o Leo!

I study Electrical Engineering in University of São Paulo (USP) in Brazil. Since I am a Japanese descendant, I came to Japan to get to know better the Japanese Culture, train my Japanese and enjoy life in this amazing country!

Origin

Brazil is a very big country, with a lot of diversity. It its most known for the tropical weather, the beaches and its beautiful landmarks like Rio de Janeiro's Christ the Redeemer, the Amazon, the Iguazu Falls, and many others. Brazilian food is really good and unique, and nowhere in the world you will find so many fruits so cheap.

I live in São Paulo, one of the biggest cities in Brazil, and is very similar to Tokyo (excluding the fact that we don't have convenience stores). The train network is not as developed, but it is good enough to use it to travel around the city.

My Hobbies

Music

I enjoy listening to music anytime of the day. I really like uplifting songs and recently I've been listening Japanese songs more. Nostalgic music and soundtracks also have a special place in my heart =).

Reading and Gaming

My two main hobbies are collecting and reading books and comics (mainly manga) and playing games. I really like a good story, either in a book, comic or a game, so my favorite genres are fiction for books, and adventure, single player games, although I also like family party games.

Scout

I've been part of the Scout Movement for almost 10 years now, and I really love it. The oportunity to learn and teach in my group is really nice and I've made a lot of friends through the places and events I've went.
