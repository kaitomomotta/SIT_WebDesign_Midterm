Origin

I came from Indonesia, a country known for it’s breathtaking natural beauty and the very diverse culture. The nature is mostly what people know Indonesia for, with beautiful beaches, mountains, and the incredible wildlife. Our diverse culture is also our strong point, as Indonesia was built from uniting different groups. This led to rich cultural heritages from different religions and traditions. To this day, hundreds of ethnics, languages, and dialects exist in Indonesia.

With all the beautiful sights one can see in Indonesia, as an Indonesian, it won’t be complete without the food. Indonesia has lots of food, some of my favorites would be rendang and pempek, I can eat them all day.
